Description:

This homemade pizza recipe is a delicious and simple way to enjoy a classic favorite in the comfort of your own kitchen. With a soft and chewy crust, topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings, this pizza is sure to be a crowd-pleaser. Whether you're feeding a large group or just looking for a quick and easy dinner option, this pizza recipe is versatile and easy to customize to your liking. So why order in when you can make a fresh, hot, and delicious pizza at home?

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F-115째F)
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 (15 oz) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, onions, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes, or until mixture becomes frothy.
  3. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil to the bowl and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes, or until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let rise in a warm place for about 30 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  6. In a small saucepan, combine the tomato sauce, basil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook over medium heat until heated through.
  7. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to the desired size and thickness. Place the dough on a large baking sheet or pizza stone.
  8. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border around the edges. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, and your desired toppings over the sauce.
