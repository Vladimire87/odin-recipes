Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description:

This spaghetti bolognese recipe is a classic Italian dish that's perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. The rich and flavorful meat sauce, made with ground beef, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs, is served over a bed of al dente spaghetti for a satisfying meal. This recipe is simple to make and will quickly become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until beef is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, pepper, and sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and return to the pot.
  4. Pour the meat sauce over the spaghetti and mix well. Serve hot with grated parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Enjoy!
